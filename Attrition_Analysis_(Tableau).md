This dashboard shows the attrition analytics of a company having several metrics such as gender, years at company, salary, age, department and so on. 
It can be seen that the overall attrition rate is around 16% while the more males have resined than the females. 
Interestingly, the employees working longer in the company as well as overall in their professional career have lower probability of leaving the company; eventhlogh with lesser salaries.
Also, Research & Development shows the highest number of outgoing people raising a particular attention while HR has the lowest attrition percentage.
This kind of dashboards are very useful for a quick and clean analysis to identify the company's manpower related concerns.
