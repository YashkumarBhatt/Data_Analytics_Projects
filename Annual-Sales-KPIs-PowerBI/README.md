# 💰 Annual Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
A quarterly Power BI dashboard tracking annual sales performance of a retail 
business across product categories, monthly profit trends, and unit pricing.

## Key Metrics Tracked
- Total profit: 58.94K INR against an estimated target of 250
- Monthly profit trends (April–June shown, Q1–Q4 switchable)
- Items sold and quantity breakdown across 10 product categories
- Unit price comparison across categories via treemap

## Key Findings
- Moisturizing products lead in both quantity sold (highest volume) and 
category share (12.73%)
- Profit shows a sharp upward trend from May to June
- Luxury and Kids categories follow closely in sales volume
- Clarifying products have the lowest sales quantity (369 units, 39% relative)

## Tool
`Power BI`
