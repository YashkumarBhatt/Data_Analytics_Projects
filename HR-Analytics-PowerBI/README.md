# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing employee attrition across departments, 
salary bands, age groups, education backgrounds, and job roles.

## Key Metrics Tracked
- Total employees, attrition count, and attrition rate (19.05%)
- Average age (38), average tenure (7.2 years), average salary (6.7K)
- Department-level filtering: Human Resources, R&D, Sales

## Key Findings
- Employees earning up to 5K salary account for the overwhelming majority 
of attrition (10 out of 12)
- The 26–35 age group shows the highest attrition count
- HR department shows 100% of attrition in the filtered view, with low 
job satisfaction scores concentrated there
- Attrition drops sharply after 5+ years at the company

## Tool & Dataset
`Power BI` · IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition dataset
