# 🌍 Global CO2 Emissions Dashboard (Tableau)

## Overview
A Tableau dashboard visualizing global CO2 emissions per capita and by region 
from 1960 to 2011, using a choropleth world map and regional trend lines.

## Key Metrics Tracked
- Average CO2 per capita (metric tons) by country — range: 0.03 to 54.42
- CO2 production (kilotons) by world region over 50+ years
- Year-by-year slider from 1960 to 2011

## Key Findings
- North America and Europe & Central Asia dominated emissions from 
1960 through the 1990s
- East Asia & Pacific surged sharply from the early 1990s onward, 
overtaking all other regions by 2011
- Sub-Saharan Africa and South Asia remain the lowest emitters 
throughout the entire period
- The US and Australia show consistently high per-capita emissions 
across all years
