# 👥 Attrition Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

## Overview
A Tableau dashboard visualizing workforce attrition patterns across gender, 
department, years at company, income levels, and total working experience.

## Key Metrics Tracked
- Overall attrition rate: 16.12%
- Attrition by gender, department, and tenure
- Income distribution mapped against years at company (scatter plot)
- Attrition decay curve across total working years

## Key Findings
- Research & Development accounts for 56.12% of total attrition — the 
largest share by department
- Sales follows at 38.82%, HR at 5.06%
- Attrition is heavily concentrated in the 0–5 year tenure bracket and 
drops sharply after 10 years
- Higher monthly income (green) correlates with longer tenure and lower 
attrition
